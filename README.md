## Overview

SpamSieve Professional Suite is a complete email filtering platform that serves power users and organizations with the training depth, analytics capability, and multi-account management needed for comprehensive spam protection across complex email setups. It extends basic spam filtering with detailed statistics, corpus management, and advanced training tools that handle the filtering challenges of high-volume email environments.

Corpus management provides visibility into and control over the statistical model that drives filtering decisions, enabling advanced users to inspect, prune, and optimize the training data that determines classification accuracy. The corpus viewer shows which words and phrases most strongly indicate spam or legitimate mail, and manual corpus editing corrects training errors that might otherwise degrade accuracy over time. Corpus export and import enable sharing of trained models between installations.

Statistics and accuracy tracking records filtering performance over time with metrics including true positive rate, false positive rate, total messages processed, and accuracy percentage that demonstrate filtering effectiveness and identify periods where accuracy might be declining. The statistics dashboard provides the data needed to assess whether the filter is performing well or needs retraining attention. Historical graphs show accuracy trends across weeks and months.

Advanced rule system combines Bayesian classification with header analysis, sender reputation, and content pattern matching that catches spam techniques specifically designed to evade statistical filters. Rules can inspect any message header, apply regular expressions to content, and combine multiple conditions with Boolean logic. The rule system complements rather than replaces the statistical engine, handling edge cases that pure Bayesian analysis might miss.

SpamSieve Professional Suite is the email filtering platform for users who process high volumes of email and need the filtering accuracy, analytical visibility, and training control that effective spam management at scale requires. Its adaptive approach ensures that filtering keeps pace with evolving spam techniques rather than becoming less effective over time.

## Performance & Integration

SpamSieve is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while SpamSieve processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
